All about Mango


The King of Fruits

A mango is a juicy stone fruit (drupe) produced from numerous species of tropical trees belonging to the flowering plant genus Mangifera, cultivated mostly for their edible fruit.

  1. Most of these species are found in nature as wild mangoes. The genus belongs to the cashew family Anacardiaceae.
  2. Mangoes are native to South Asia,from where the "common mango" or "Indian mango",
  3. Mangifera indica, has been distributed worldwide to become one of the most widely cultivated fruits in the tropics.
  4. Other Mangifera species (e.g. horse mango, Mangifera foetida) are grown on a more localized basis.
